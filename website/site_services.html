Shaurya Martial Arts Programs

Empowering through discipline, strength, and safety.

Traditional Goju-Ryu Karate Training

Dive into the authentic teachings of Goju-Ryu Karate, an Okinawan martial art emphasizing physical fitness and mental discipline. This comprehensive approach empowers students with practical self-defense techniques while building inner strength and resilience.

  • Kihon (Basics): Master foundational techniques of strikes, blocks, kicks, and stances.
  • Kata (Forms): Engage in pre-arranged sequences simulating combat, teaching strategy, timing, and precision.
  • Kumite (Sparring): Controlled sparring to hone reflexes, timing, distance control, and mutual respect.

Suraksha Setu Training Program

A self-defense program for government school students, focused on educating and raising awareness about personal safety. It teaches vital self-defense techniques and helps students recognize and react to potential threats effectively.

  • Safety Awareness: Fosters a sense of security and preparedness.
  • Promote Safety: Equips students to handle critical situations with confidence.
